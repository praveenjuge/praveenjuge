I gave Codex the URL of an old website and asked it to move the site from Cloudflare Pages to Cloudflare Workers. That was it. No repo link. No long migration brief. Just the URL.

It found the GitHub repo, cloned it locally, converted the site from Hugo to Astro, pushed the changes to GitHub, deleted the Cloudflare Pages version, deployed the new site to Cloudflare Workers, and assigned the domain to it.

The whole thing took about 20 minutes. Everything worked. No weird cleanup. No half-broken step in the middle. Just done.

That is the shift I keep coming back to. Work that used to feel like a careful half-day project now starts to feel like a sentence. A clear request. A result. I love codex.
